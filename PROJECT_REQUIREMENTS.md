# NMB Website Project Requirements

## Website Goals
*   **Presenting NMB as a modern, digital-first bank:** Showcasing technological advancement and ease of use.
*   **Access to Key Services:** Making it easy for customers to find Accounts, Loans, and other essential banking services.
*   **Focus on Sustainability and Innovation:** Highlighting the bank's commitment to ESG and future-forward solutions.
*   **Driving Conversions:** Encouraging account openings, loan inquiries, and app downloads.
*   **Offering Useful Content:** Providing easy access to FAQs, financial literacy resources, and customer support.

## Key User Personas
*   **Retail Customer:** Looking for personal banking solutions like accounts, cards, or loans.
*   **Business Client:** Exploring corporate services, POS solutions, or trade finance.
*   **Investor/Partner:** Looking for sustainability reports, financial results, and governance information.
*   **Keen Customer/General User:** Looking for general information, FAQs, help, or latest news.

## Ideal User Paths
*   **Retail Customer:** Homepage → Banking for Me → Accounts
*   **Business Client:** Homepage → Business Accounts
*   **Investor/Partner:** Homepage → Investor Relations → Annual Report
*   **Keen Customer:** Homepage → Help Center / News / Contact Us

## Content Structure & Layout

### Homepage Structure
1.  **Hero Banner:** Rotating visuals focusing on lifestyle and digital banking.
2.  **Quick Action Buttons:** Open Account, Download App, Contact.
3.  **Offerings Highlights:** Key products and services.
4.  **News & Updates:** Latest announcements.
5.  **CSR / Sustainability Highlight:** Community and environmental focus.
